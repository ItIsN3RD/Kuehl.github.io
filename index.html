About Us

Powering Excellence Since 1971

Kuehl Electric, Inc. started contracting in 1971, and, in its second generation of family ownership incorporated in 1992. Kuehl Electric, Inc. takes pride in being a customer oriented supplier of electrical installations and services. We work hard to exceed our customer’s expectations, and aim to deliver high-quality workmanship, on time, every time. Thanks to our commitments, we enjoy a large and loyal customer base through out Wisconsin and the upper Midwest.

Industrial It’ll blow your mind.

Kuehl Electric INC specializes in industrial services, offering expertise in various sectors including foundries, machine shops, manufacturing plants, municipal water facilities, paper mills, power generation, and facility power distribution. They provide a range of services such as buss duct installation, machine feeder setups, A/C and D/C drive installations, MCC (Motor Control Center) installations, medium voltage substations, custom machine control solutions, and PLC (Programmable Logic Controller) programming and integration.

Commercial See for yourself.

Kuehl Electric INC specializes in commercial services, providing electrical solutions for a diverse range of clients including airports, churches, financial institutions, food processing facilities, medical centers, office buildings, and retail stores. Their services encompass installation and maintenance of air conditioning and heating systems, lighting and control systems, new construction wiring, remodeling projects, UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) systems, and low voltage systems.
